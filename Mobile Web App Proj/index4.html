About Us

Indian Student Association is a Indian club at UA Little Rock. It represents the many Indian Students in the UA Little Rock community. The purpose of ISA is to unite students and expand their knowledge about different Indian culture by making International students feel at home on US Soil.

The organization give students opportunity to merge into Indian culture and able to express their Indian heritage by paticipating in Indian festivals.
